How it works

Create Offer

Client submits seller username, budget, timeline, description, and attachments.

Seller Accepts

Seller accepts terms and offer inside Telegram bot.

Crypto Payment

Client accepts escrow terms, selects USDT, USDC, or BTC, then uploads TX hash.

Work Countdown

Deadline starts after payment confirmation. Bot sends reminders every 12 hours.

Submit & Review

Seller submits work. Client approves, requests revision, disputes, or cancels.

Release Funds

Funds release only after client approval or admin final decision.

Management Flows Included

Buyer & Seller Protection

Terms acceptance, escrow payment, revision limits, refund wallet collection, auto-release review window, disputes, and ratings.

Admin Control

Manual seller assignment, payment confirmation, pause project, extend deadline, resolve disputes, refunds, seller release, service/category management, and referral tracking.
